# ADR-0040: Add face, workshop, and garden word meshes

Date: 2026-07-18
Status: accepted

## Decision
Add eighteen first-class easy concepts for visible face anatomy, familiar hand tools,
and garden equipment, with 69 collision-screened inflections and 54 explicit directed
semantic edges. Use three strongly connected local meshes with exactly two outgoing
choices per new node. Point one legacy bridge into every new concept and no edge back to
the legacy graph. Qualify `Ghiveci de flori` and `Furtun de grădină`; keep their broad
bare forms and other neighboring senses outside the new ownership. Add no board or
promotion.

## Context / why
These concrete beginner terms had no honest resolver owner. Conservative ordinary
inflections make input forgiving, while qualified garden labels avoid claiming broader
container and tube senses. `Nară` replaces the initially considered `Bărbie`, whose
accent-folded spelling would reserve `barbie`. Both accepted `fierăstrău` and
`ferăstrău` families belong to one physical-saw concept; older and regional spellings
remain blocked. Isolated spokes would be weak Cald sau Rece targets, while edges from a
new mesh back into the mature graph could change old shortest paths.

## Consequences
The fixture becomes 2,269 nodes, 9,068 edges, 7,333 aliases, and 180 legacy puzzles;
combined eligible beginner probes resolve 304/304. Every new target is reachable from
2,222 concepts and has 359–1,415 responsive guesses within five hops. The one-way sink
cut preserves every legacy Contexto, Lanț, and Alchimie profile; deterministic rebuilding
changes three science legacy puzzles, one of which now targets `Buză`. Both critique
reports and the 794-record pack remain unchanged; the public mobile contract moves to
`fixture-v32-face-workshop-garden`. Session TTL and caps do not change.
