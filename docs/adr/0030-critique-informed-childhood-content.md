# ADR-0030: Stage a critique-informed childhood content wave

Date: 2026-07-16
Status: accepted

## Decision

Add a bounded graph wave around cross-generational childhood games, first-school objects,
classic Ion Creangă stories, Petre Ispirescu tales, and Romanian fairy-tale archetypes.
Stage all seven derived game items as `pending`: one Conexiuni board, two Contexto targets,
two Lanț boards, and two Alchimie boards. Require the exact seven-item deterministic critique
to be clean before handoff, but do not promote any item without ADR-0023's analyst plus
adversarial-verifier gate artifact.

## Context / why

The critique research favors recognizable concepts, legible progress, meaningful route/craft
choices, and strict Conexiuni predicates. The existing pack instead has 62 nodes already over
the Conexiuni member-use warning and many repeated quads, while schoolyard play and classic
children's reading were sparse. A [Romanian school manual](https://www.scgimpngtitu.ro/pdf/10.12.2024/MANUALE%20DIGITALE%202024-2025/5%20ROM.pdf)
explicitly groups Rațele și vânătorii, Telefonul fără fir, Moară/Țintar, Nu te supăra,
frate!, and Piticot as familiar games; contemporary Romanian coverage also groups Elasticul,
Șotronul and Țară, țară, vrem ostași as childhood play. The literary claims are grounded in the primary texts and durable
catalogue evidence for [Prâslea](https://ro.wikisource.org/wiki/Pr%C3%A2slea_cel_voinic_%C8%99i_merele_de_aur),
[Greuceanu](https://ro.wikisource.org/wiki/Greuceanu), and
[Ion Creangă's stories](https://www.romania-actualitati.ro/articole-despre/ion%2Bcreanga/).

Why not regenerate the unavailable v22 scratch payload: only its count summary remains, so
reconstructing it would pretend to preserve facts and judgments that are no longer auditable.
Why not make Moară and Țintar separate tiles: they are names for the same game, which would
create duplicate concepts and a false Conexiuni group. Why not approve the new items directly:
mechanical cleanliness does not settle recognition, predicate honesty, or bounded-choice
quality, and version-2 review bindings must cover the final graph.

## Consequences

The fixture gains 22 nodes, 78 non-distractor edges, and 25 aliases. The seven new pack items
remain unserved pending the subjective gate; their exact deterministic critique reports zero
WARN and zero FAIL findings. Conexiuni uses four type-coherent, checkable groups with no raw or
type-compatible cross-group unfairness. Both Lanț boards have two shortest-path first hops and
no choke layer; both Contexto targets have five or more strong recognizable neighbors; both
Alchimie dossiers expose multiple productive openings and a bounded minimum-action recipe.

The denser graph re-derives the legacy terminal puzzles and existing graph-dependent pack
values through the standard importer. Runtime selection, hidden-answer boundaries, APIs,
scoring, two-hour sliding session TTL, and the 1,000-entry per-game session cap are unchanged.
